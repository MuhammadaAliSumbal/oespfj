OBJECTIVES

Oxford Education System is committed to develop the community through manpower which is skilled, have the vision, have the skill and have the practical knowledge of the content normally which is given in the theory. Oxford Education System is trying to launch the change in the community through the practical implication of the theoretical knowledge.

Capacity
building

Capacity building and training is the main focus of Oxford Education System. Oxford Education System is trying to build the capacity of the teachers by training. All the people who have the theoretical knowledge and they are even the fresh graduates and non-graduates and the community members (parents, mothers, and social activitist). They are invited in the training sessions and they are given awareness about the teaching learning process. The fresh graduates were equipped with the modern techniques of importing the knowledge and the teaching skills, teaching strategies, and all the activities which are needed to develop a unique and developmental and positive nation.
